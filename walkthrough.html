Build the Quote

Erica clicks the "PO Quote" bookmark in Chrome. A popup asks for the deal number — she types it in and hits OK. The quote builder opens with everything pulled from HubSpot: customer name, email, and all line items.

She edits line items, adds or removes rows, fills in the school name, attention line, mailing address, and any comments for the school's finance department. Totals update automatically.

3
Customer
Reviews, Edits & Finalizes Their Quote

The customer clicks the link in their email and lands on their own quote dashboard. This is the self-service tool — it looks like a formal document branded "Trimsuits by Show Off Inc." with all the line items Erica set up.

✎ The customer can edit presentation fields — quote title, company name, attention line, mailing address. This lets them reformat the quote to match their school's procurement system. Line items and prices are locked — only Erica can change those.

At the bottom of the page, the customer:

1. Enters their PO Number (if they have one already)
2. Chooses who receives the formal quote — they can change the email and add CC recipients (their finance department, athletic director, etc.)
3. Clicks "Finalize Quote Template"

This creates a formal HubSpot quote and emails it to whoever the customer chose. The customer routes it to the right people at their school themselves — we don't have to play middleman.

4
Customer
Uploads Their PO Document

After finalizing, the page shows a success screen with a PO Upload section. The customer drags and drops their PO document (PDF, PNG, or JPG, max 3MB) and clicks "Upload PO".

The file gets saved directly to the deal in HubSpot. Both the customer and our team get confirmation emails at support@showoffinc.com.

📋 Not every customer uploads right away. Schools can take weeks to issue a PO. The upload link stays active — they come back from the email anytime. The deal status stays at "Finalized" until they upload.
👀 Team note: When a PO is uploaded, a notification hits the HubSpot inbox automatically. The PO document is linked on the deal record — no need to ask the customer to email it separately.
5
Automatic
Everything Updates in HubSpot

At every stage, the system automatically updates the deal in HubSpot — PO quote status, PO number, PO document link, and formal quote link. Notification emails go to support@showoffinc.com at each stage, so the whole team has visibility without needing to check the deal.

Common Questions
What if the customer needs to change pricing?
They click "Request Changes" on the review page, which opens an email to support@showoffinc.com. That lands in our inbox. Erica adjusts line items on the admin page and re-sends the review link.
Can the customer send the quote to someone else at the school?
Yes — that's the whole point. When they finalize, they choose who gets the formal quote email and can add CC recipients. Finance department, athletic director, procurement office — they route it themselves. We never have to be the go-between.
What if the customer doesn't upload a PO right away?
Totally normal. Schools can take weeks to issue a PO. The link in the email stays active indefinitely — they come back whenever they're ready. The deal status stays at "Finalized" until they upload.
How does the team know when a PO is uploaded?
A notification email hits support@showoffinc.com automatically. The PO document is also linked directly on the HubSpot deal record — no need to ask the customer to email it separately.
Can Erica re-send or edit a quote after it's been sent?
Yes. She opens the admin page with the bookmark, makes changes, and clicks "Send to Customer" again. The customer gets a fresh email with the updated review link.
How long is the quote valid?
120 days from the date it's sent. After that, the formal quote expires and Erica would send a new one.
Where is the formal quote document?
After the customer finalizes, HubSpot hosts a branded quote page they can print or forward. The link is saved on the deal as "PO Quote Link" and also emailed to the customer and whoever they CC'd.
