Every custom costume order follows the same steps, whether you're ordering for a full team or a solo performer. Most of the work is on us — you'll share your vision, submit measurements, approve your design, and we take it from there.

1
You
Place Your Order

Start by filling out our order form. You'll tell us about yourself, your sport, how many costumes you need, and your design vision. Upload any reference images — inspiration photos, logos, photos of existing costumes, anything that helps us understand what you're looking for.

💡 One order per costume design. If your team needs two different looks (say, a competition costume and a warm-up), place a separate order for each design. Multiple quantities of the same design go in one order.
showoffinc.com/start-your-order
This is our real order form — scroll inside to preview each step

The form takes about 10 minutes. After you submit, you'll get a confirmation email, your measurement form link, and our design team gets started right away.

📷 The more detail you give, the faster your sketch is ready. Thorough intake submissions with clear reference images often mean your sketch can be finalized and approved without a design call. A few extra minutes here can save days later.
📅 Want to talk through your vision? At the end of the form, you can book a Zoom call with our designer. It's completely optional — in many cases the designer may already come to the call with a sketch prepared to review together. But if you have a complex design or want to brainstorm ideas, it's a great option.

Submit Measurements

Right after you place your order, you'll receive an email with a link to our measurement form. This is your most important next step — submit measurements as carefully and as soon as possible so every costume fits perfectly and production can start on time.

Open Measurement Form

You can save your progress and come back anytime.
⚠ Don't wait on this. Measurements are needed before we can start production. The sooner you submit, the sooner your costumes can be made. Take your time getting them right — but don't put it off.

You can fill it out yourself, or share the link with parents, a coach, or anyone helping. The form saves automatically as you go — no need to finish everything in one sitting.

📏 What you'll need: A flexible measuring tape and a partner to help. The form will show you exactly which measurements are needed for your specific costume design — every design is different, so just follow what the form asks for.
💬 If any measurements look unusual, we'll always double-check and reach out before moving forward. Additional measurements may be requested later depending on your design. Please be open to re-measuring if we ask — it's all about getting the best fit.

Common Questions
How long does the whole process take?
It depends on the complexity of your design, how quickly measurements and payment come in, the time of year, and how much we currently have on our production schedule. Once we have your approved sketch, measurements, and payment, production typically takes a few weeks. We'll keep you updated at every stage.
Can I make changes after I place my order?
Yes — until your sketch is approved, design changes are always welcome. After approval, changes may affect your timeline, so reach out to us as early as possible. We'll always work with you to get it right.
What if I need to change the number of costumes?
Just email us at support@showoffinc.com. We can adjust the quantity on your order. If measurements have already been submitted, we'll update the form so you can add new performers.
Can someone else fill out the measurement form?
Absolutely. Share the link from the email with a coach, parents, or anyone helping with measurements. Multiple people can contribute — the form saves progress automatically.
What if a measurement is wrong?
Email us and we'll re-open your form so you can correct it. It's important to get measurements right — we'd rather take a few extra minutes to double-check than have a costume that doesn't fit.
Do you make costumes for solo performers?
Yes! Solos, duets, trios — we make custom costumes for any size group, even just one. The process is exactly the same.
What if I already know what I want from the catalog?
We have a catalog of ready-made designs you can order directly. The process is the same, but the sketch step is already done — you'll go straight to measurements and payment after placing your order.
Do you offer 50/50 payment?
Yes — for larger orders, we can split your payment into two installments. Just let us know when you're ready to pay and we'll set it up.
Do I have to pay shipping separately for each design?
If you're ordering multiple designs at the same time, each design is a separate order with its own shipping. This is because different designs may finish production at different times.
Do you accept purchase orders (POs)?
Yes! We work with schools and organizations that require PO-based purchasing. We'll set up a formal quote you can send to your procurement department, and they can submit their PO document directly through our system.
Do you do alterations?
Yes — we offer alteration services for costumes we've made. If something doesn't fit right or you need adjustments after receiving your order, email us at support@showoffinc.com and we'll walk you through the process.
What if the costume doesn't fit?
We take every measurement carefully and double-check anything that looks unusual. If something still doesn't fit after delivery, reach out to us — we'll work with you on alterations to make it right.
How do I contact you if I have questions?
Email us anytime at support@showoffinc.com. We're a small team and we respond personally — no chatbots, no ticket numbers.
